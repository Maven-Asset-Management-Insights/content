[Work orders](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo) are at the center of how work is planned, executed, and recorded in Maximo.

They capture what was done, how it was done, and what was observed. When this data is complete and consistent, it becomes one of the most valuable sources of insight for improving reliability.

If you have seen how [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality) impacts reliability overall, work orders are where that connection becomes most visible.

---

## Work Orders Connect Planning and Execution

[Work orders](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo) bring together several key elements:

- Asset information
- [Job plans](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#job-plan)
- Labor and materials
- [Failure details](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#failure-code)
- Completion notes

Because they sit at the intersection of these elements, they play a central role in overall [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality).

---

## Where Consistency Creates Value

Small improvements in how [work orders](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo) are completed can significantly improve [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality) over time.

Key areas to focus on include:

### Complete and Consistent Data Entry

Ensuring required fields are filled out consistently helps create a reliable dataset and is the foundation of strong [work order data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-data-quality).

### Alignment with Actual Work

Work orders are most valuable when they accurately reflect what happened in the field. This [execution alignment](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#execution-alignment) is what separates data that informs decisions from data that simply records activity.

### Clear and Useful Descriptions

Well-written descriptions provide context that structured fields alone cannot capture, supporting [closeout quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#closeout-quality) and long-term maintenance history.

---

## How This Supports Reliability

When [work order](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo) data is consistent and aligned with execution:

- [Failure patterns](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#failure-mode) become easier to identify
- Maintenance history becomes more reliable, supporting stronger [data confidence](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-confidence)
- [Planning](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#planning-and-scheduling) decisions become more informed
- [Asset performance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#asset-performance) tracking becomes more accurate

Reliable data leads to more confident decisions.

---

## Connecting to Other Data Elements

[Work order closeout quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-closeout-quality) is closely tied to other areas of Maximo data:

- **[Failure coding](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#failure-code)** provides structured insight into issues
- **[Asset hierarchy](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#asset-hierarchy)** ensures work is tied to the correct assets
- **[Job plans](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#job-plan)** define how work should be performed

When these elements are aligned, [work orders](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo) become a strong, connected source of [usable data](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#usable-data).

## Strengthening Work Order Data Quality

Improvement starts with a few practical steps:

- Define required fields for [work order closeout](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-closeout)
- Align data entry with [operational data alignment](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#operational-data-alignment) standards
- Provide guidance on clear descriptions to improve [closeout quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#closeout-quality)
- Reinforce standards through [data governance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-governance) and regular review
- Assign ownership for maintaining [data discipline](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-discipline)

These steps help create consistency without adding unnecessary complexity.

## From Activity to Insight

[Work orders](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo) are more than records of completed tasks — they are a continuous source of operational insight.

When [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality) is strong, they provide a clear picture of [asset performance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#asset-performance), support better [planning](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#planning-and-scheduling), and enable more targeted [reliability](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#reliability-strategy) improvements.

This is where data quality becomes visible in day-to-day operations.

---

## Building Toward Better Outcomes

Improving [work order data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-data-quality) is not about adding more data. It is about improving the quality and consistency of what is already being captured.

When [work orders](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-wo), [failure coding](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#failure-code), [asset hierarchy](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#asset-hierarchy), and [job plans](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#job-plan) are aligned, organizations create a reliable foundation for better decisions and stronger [asset performance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#asset-performance).
