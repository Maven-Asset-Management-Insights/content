Of the five stages of asset lifecycle management, maintenance is by far the longest. It's also where the gap between what organizations intend and what they actually do is widest.

## Scheduled maintenance is a starting point, not a strategy.

A [PM program](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#preventive-maintenance-pm) keeps assets serviced. It doesn't tell you whether the intervals are right, whether failures are recurring for a reason, or whether the maintenance cost trajectory is sustainable. Those questions require something more: [condition-based monitoring](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#condition-monitoring), [failure pattern analysis](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#failure-mode), and a feedback loop between what happens in the field and how the [reliability strategy](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#reliability-strategy) evolves.

Modern [EAM](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#enterprise-asset-management-eam) platforms like Maximo make that feedback loop possible — [IoT](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#internet-of-things-iot) sensor integration, [predictive analytics](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#predictive-analytics), historical failure data all in one environment. But the technology only works when the [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality) feeding it is structured and trustworthy.

That means consistent [failure coding](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#failure-code). Accurate [work order closeout](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-order-closeout). [Job plans](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#job-plan) that reflect how work is actually performed. Without that foundation, [predictive maintenance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#predictive-maintenance-pdm) becomes predictive guessing.

The maintenance stage is long enough that small [data discipline](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-discipline) gaps compound into major reliability problems. Build the foundation first.
