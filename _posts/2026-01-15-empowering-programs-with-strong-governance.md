## Empowering Programs with Strong Governance for Lasting Impact

Most Maximo and reliability initiatives start strong and then slowly fade.

The difference between lasting value and stalled progress is [governance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-governance).

---

## The Familiar Pattern

Organizations invest heavily in:

- Maximo upgrades
- reliability assessments
- [PM optimization](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#preventive-maintenance-pm)
- new reporting

Six months later:

- standards loosen
- [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality) slips
- work practices drift
- leadership attention moves elsewhere

The system remains.  
The discipline does not.

---

## Why Governance Is Often Misunderstood

Governance is not bureaucracy.

Effective [data governance](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-governance):

- clarifies ownership
- reinforces standards
- creates visibility
- sustains discipline

It answers a simple question:

> "Who ensures this stays true next quarter?"

---

## What Effective Governance Looks Like

Successful programs establish a predictable rhythm:

### Monthly

- [data quality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-quality) checks
- [KPI](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#key-performance-indicator-kpi) review
- exceptions discussed

### Quarterly

- [PM effectiveness](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#preventive-maintenance-pm-compliance) review
- [reliability strategy](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#reliability-strategy) updates
- [asset criticality](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#asset-criticality) validation

### Annually

- [data standard enforcement](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-standard-enforcement) refresh
- roadmap alignment
- capability reassessment

Governance is lightweight, but relentless.

---

## How Maximo Supports Governance

Maximo enables governance when:

- standards are configured, not implied
- workflows reinforce [work management discipline](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-management-discipline)
- reporting aligns to [KPIs](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#key-performance-indicator-kpi) that leaders actually use for decisions

Without governance, Maximo becomes passive. [Data drift](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-drift) sets in quietly — and by the time it's visible, the cost of recovery is significant.

---

## Maven Perspective

Governance is what turns improvement into habit.

It's the difference between knowing what good looks like, and staying there. That staying power depends on [data discipline](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#data-discipline), [work management consistency](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#work-management-consistency), and a [reliability strategy](https://maven-asset-management-insights.github.io/content/glossary/#reliability-strategy) that is reviewed and reinforced — not just written once and forgotten.
