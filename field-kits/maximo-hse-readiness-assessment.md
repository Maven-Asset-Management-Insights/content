## The Hard Questions Worth Asking

If you're already running Maximo, this assessment is as much about what you're not using as what you are. A few questions worth sitting with:

- Are incident reports living in spreadsheets or email chains instead of a managed system?
- Does your permit-to-work process have full documentation and traceability?
- Could you demonstrate regulatory compliance to an auditor today — without scrambling?
- Do you know which workers have expiring certifications before they show up for a job?

If the answer to any of these is "not sure," Maximo HS&E may be the missing piece.

> **Key insight:** Maximo HS&E isn't a separate safety system. It integrates directly into MAS, putting incident management, permits, MOC, and compliance tracking inside the workflows your team already uses. When safety lives in the same place as your work orders and asset records, doing things the right way stops being extra work — it's just how the work gets done.
